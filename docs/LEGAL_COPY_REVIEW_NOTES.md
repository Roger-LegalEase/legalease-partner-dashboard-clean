# Legal Copy Review Notes

## Disclaimers Added
- RecordShield is for personal self-review.
- RecordShield is not for employment, tenant, credit, insurance, or eligibility decisions.
- RecordShield does not guarantee expungement, sealing, correction, or record removal.
- AI-generated summaries may contain errors or omissions.
- Users should verify results and consult a qualified attorney for legal advice.
- Background-check data may depend on provider availability, jurisdiction, court data, and reporting rules.
- Monitoring does not guarantee detection of every record or change.
- The product is in beta and may change.

## Risky Language Removed Or Reduced
- Customer-facing references to eligibility results were changed to readiness language.
- Definitive user-facing "likely eligible" style copy was changed to "may be ready" or manual review language.
- Customer-facing copy avoids approval, denial, scoring, ranking, hireability, and suitability claims.

## Known Beta Limitations
- Provider availability, jurisdiction, court data, and reporting rules may affect results.
- AI summaries can fail, omit context, or require manual review.
- Monitoring may not detect every record or change.
- Data deletion/anonymization may preserve non-personal audit, billing, or compliance records where appropriate.
