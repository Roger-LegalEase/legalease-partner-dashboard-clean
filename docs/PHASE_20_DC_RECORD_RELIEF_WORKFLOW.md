# Phase 20: DC Record Relief Workflow

RCAP now supports a District of Columbia branch alongside Mississippi and Illinois.

Source files:

- `reference/dc/DC-Expungement-Sealing-Agent-Reference.pdf`
- `reference/dc/dc-motion-to-seal-expunge.html`

DC output uses motion pleadings, not checkbox court forms. The packet includes a motion to seal or expunge, statement of points and authorities, proposed order, certificate of service, and filing instructions.

Automatic DC relief is described carefully as possible or scheduled relief. The workflow does not treat automatic processing as completed record clearing.
