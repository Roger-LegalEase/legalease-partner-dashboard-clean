# Wilma Known Limitations

Wilma determines whether a user appears to fit LegalEase's self-help document-preparation workflow. Wilma does not guarantee expungement, sealing, nondisclosure, court approval, or legal eligibility.

## Current Supported States

- Illinois
- Pennsylvania
- Maryland
- District of Columbia
- Mississippi
- Texas

## Disabled Automated Paid Paths

- IL conviction sealing.
- PA felony limited access.
- MD guilty-disposition expungement.
- DC actual innocence and conviction sealing.
- MS felony expunction and trafficking-victim relief.
- TX nondisclosure.

## Out Of Scope

- Federal cases.
- Juvenile cases in the adult flow.
- Pending criminal cases for paid automation.
- Legal advice, strategy, guarantees, or court predictions.
- Collateral-consequence advice, including immigration, firearms, licensing, custody, or employment-law advice.

## Operational Limits

- Free screening has a message cap.
- Sessions expire after inactivity.
- Repeated screening may be throttled by email, IP, or device.
- Bot-protection failures are blocked.
- Paid fulfillment is triggered only by verified payment-provider webhook events.
