How to file (Philadelphia)

You're ready. Here's exactly what to do.

These steps are specific to the Philadelphia Court of Common Pleas, where your case was heard.

1
Print your packet
Print the 3 documents below, single-sided. You'll need the original plus 2 copies of each.
2
Take them to the Clerk of Courts
1301 Filbert St, Room 100, Philadelphia. Open Mon, Fri 8:30am, 4:30pm. Bring a photo ID.
3
Ask to file an expungement petition
Hand the clerk your packet. If you can't afford the filing fee, ask for a fee waiver form, we've included guidance on this.
4
Keep your stamped copy
The clerk will stamp your copies. Keep one safe, then come back here and mark it filed.
