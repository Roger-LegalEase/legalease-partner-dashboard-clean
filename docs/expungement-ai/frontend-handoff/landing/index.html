Questions

The things people ask before they start

Is this actually legit, or too good to be true?

It's legit, and it's legal. You're allowed to clear your own record without a lawyer; most people just never do because the paperwork and court steps are confusing. We prepare the same documents a lawyer would prepare and walk you through filing them yourself. That's why it's $50 instead of thousands: you do the filing, we do the hard part.

Will this work for my record and my state?

It depends on your charges and your state's law, which is exactly what the free eligibility check is for. Screening and guidance are available for all 50 states plus DC. Packet and payment availability depends on your result and jurisdiction, so answer a few plain questions and we'll tell you honestly where you stand before you pay anything.

Are you lawyers? Is this legal advice?

No. Expungement.ai is not a law firm and does not give legal advice or represent you. We provide self-help document preparation and plain-language guidance, built and reviewed by a criminal defense attorney in every state we serve. If you want someone to advise you on your specific situation, you should talk to a licensed attorney.

What if my petition gets denied?

The free check is designed to flag cases that aren't likely to qualify before you spend a dollar, so you're not paying to file something that won't land. No one can promise a court outcome, and we won't pretend otherwise. What we can do is make sure your paperwork is complete, correct, and filed the way your court expects.

When do I pay, and what does the $50 cover?

Nothing until you're ready to file. Checking eligibility and seeing your options is free. The $50 is one flat price per case and covers all your prepared court documents and step-by-step filing instructions for your specific court. Courts may charge their own filing fee; many will waive it, and we show you how to ask.

Is my information private?

Yes. What you share stays private and is never sold. You don't even need an account to check if you qualify.
