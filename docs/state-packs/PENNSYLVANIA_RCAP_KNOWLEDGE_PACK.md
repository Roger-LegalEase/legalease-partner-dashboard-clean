# Pennsylvania RCAP Knowledge Pack

Phase 21 adds Pennsylvania-only record relief support to the universal RCAP journey.

Pennsylvania uses three mechanisms: expungement under 18 Pa.C.S. § 9122, petition-based limited access / sealing under 18 Pa.C.S. § 9122.1, and automatic Clean Slate sealing under 18 Pa.C.S. § 9122.2.

Workflow guardrails:

- Expungement means destruction and is narrow.
- Limited access / sealing hides records from most public searches but does not destroy them.
- Clean Slate is automatic limited access where applicable and must not be described as already complete.
- Convictions generally route to sealing / limited access, not expungement, unless a narrow pardon, age 70+, ARD, summary, or non-conviction expungement path applies.
- PATCH report review is central because data, alias, docket, and agency gaps can cause missed records.
