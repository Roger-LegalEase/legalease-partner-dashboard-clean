Questions

The things people ask before they start

Is this actually legit?
Yes. People are often allowed to file record-clearing paperwork themselves. Expungement.ai is a self-help document preparation product: we screen your answers, prepare paperwork, and guide you through filing. We are not a law firm and do not represent you.
Do I need a lawyer?
Not always. Many people can file self-help petitions on their own, depending on their case and state. If your situation is complex, risky, or unclear, we'll tell you when speaking with a licensed attorney may be the better next step.
Will this work in my state?
Start with the free eligibility check. We screen for all 50 states + DC, but packet availability depends on your case, court, and jurisdiction.

The $50 per case covers your prepared documents, filing checklist, court-specific instructions, Briefcase access, and Wilma guidance. Court filing fees, if any, are separate.
What if my petition is denied?
No one can promise a court outcome. Our goal is to help you avoid filing something that clearly does not qualify and to make your packet complete, organized, and ready to file.

Yes. You can begin without creating an account, and your information is never sold.
Are you a law firm?
No. Expungement.ai provides self-help document preparation and general filing guidance. We do not provide legal advice, represent you, or file on your behalf.
