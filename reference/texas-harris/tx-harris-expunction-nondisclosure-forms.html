Order of Expunction Order of Nondisclosure

On this day the Court considered the Petition for Expunction of Criminal Records filed under Chapter 55A of the Code of Criminal Procedure. for an Order of Nondisclosure filed under Government Code Ch. 411, Subch. E-1. The Court, having reviewed the petition and the applicable law, finds that Petitioner is entitled to the relief requested.

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that each agency named in the petition EXPUNGE all records and files relating to the arrest of Petitioner described above, by destroying or returning such records as required by Chapter 55A, and that the Texas Department of Public Safety give effect to this Order as provided by law.

IT IS THEREFORE ORDERED that the Texas Department of Public Safety SEAL and prohibit disclosure of the criminal history record information relating to the offense described above, except to the agencies and for the purposes enumerated in Government Code § 411.075(b), and that DPS notify those agencies of this Order.

IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the relief granted applies to the arrest described in the petition and the agencies named therein.
