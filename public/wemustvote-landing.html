Questions before you start

Still unsure? That is normal.

Record-clearing can feel confusing. This screening is meant to give you a simple, private first step before you decide what to do next.

Who is this for?+

People in Mississippi who have an arrest, charge, conviction, dismissal, or old record and want to understand possible record-clearing options.

Is this free to start?+

Yes. The guided screening is free to start.

Do I need court papers to start?+

No. Court papers are helpful, but you can begin with what you know.

Who is Wilma?+

Wilma is LegalEase's guided intake assistant. She asks basic questions and helps point you toward possible next steps.

Will this automatically clear my record?+

No. This is a first step. Depending on your situation, you may move to record review, expungement support, clinic review, or another pathway.

What if I am not eligible right now?+

You may still receive information about future updates or other available support.

Is LegalEase a law firm?+

No. LegalEase is not a law firm and does not provide legal advice. LegalEase provides legal information, guided screening, and document support where available.
